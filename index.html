The verticality and multi-lane structure added layers of complexity to the game, allowing players to explore diverse strategies while navigating between the lanes. My focus on infrastructure ensured the map was optimized for smooth performance, integrating collision detection, asset loading, and environmental interactions seamlessly into the game engine.The map's alien forest setting further showcases my capacity to conceptualize and implement unique environments that enhance a game’s narrative and aesthetic appeal. I employed level design principles to balance challenge and accessibility, ensuring that players of varying skill levels could engage meaningfully with the gameplay. Collaborating closely with the development team, I ensured that the map aligned with the overall game mechanics and art style while meeting production deadlines. This project highlights my ability to design, develop, and implement game architecture that supports a complete game project, reflecting technical expertise and creativity in equal measure.

Project: Tank_World

In developing a tank game, I implemented and analyzed fundamental data structures and algorithms to enhance gameplay mechanics, particularly in crafting advanced AI behaviors. Each tank in the game was programmed with distinct personality types, ranging from aggressive and tactical to defensive and cautious. This required designing algorithms that dictated decision-making processes, pathfinding, and dynamic responses to player actions. To achieve this, I utilized data structures such as state machines to manage AI states and priority queues to ensure efficient decision-making during real-time gameplay. These implementations not only created engaging and unpredictable opponents but also demonstrated my ability to leverage core computational concepts to elevate the player experience. The project involved extensive analysis and optimization to ensure smooth performance, even with multiple AI tanks operating simultaneously. By integrating algorithms like A* for pathfinding and adaptive heuristics for decision-making, I was able to create tanks that reacted intelligently to in-game scenarios, such as flanking, retreating, or coordinating attacks. This attention to detail in gameplay mechanics required iterative testing and refinement, as well as balancing the AI personalities to provide both challenge and fairness. Through this project, I showcased my ability to apply fundamental data structures and algorithms in a practical, interactive context, demonstrating a deep understanding of their role in supporting complex gameplay systems.

Project: Inbetween-Worlds

In the development of a third-person shooter set within a richly detailed, multi-world environment, I utilized software development processes to analyze project requirements and craft an innovative solution. The unique premise of the game—a map that merges disparate worlds—posed a challenging design problem, requiring in-depth analysis to ensure cohesion in both gameplay and aesthetics. I began by breaking down the project into key components, identifying core gameplay mechanics, environmental interactions, and the narrative-driven elements that tied the worlds together. Using agile development methodologies, I iteratively designed and built the game, employing modular programming to separate and streamline mechanics such as player movement, aiming systems, and combat interactions. Thorough testing was integral to the success of the project. I implemented a test-driven development approach to identify and resolve potential issues early, ensuring robust collision systems, seamless transitions between world zones, and responsive player controls. Each test phase provided valuable feedback, allowing me to refine gameplay mechanics and optimize performance. By combining analytical problem-solving with practical software development tools, I was able to design, build, and deliver a third-person shooter that not only met the project goals but also provided a compelling, immersive experience for players. This project demonstrates my ability to apply structured development processes to tackle complex game design challenges effectively.

I implemented modular code structures to support scalability, enabling seamless addition of new levels and mechanics. Testing and debugging in a new environment required a methodical approach, enhancing my ability to troubleshoot and refine under different constraints. By successfully creating a cohesive and enjoyable platformer in GameMaker, I demonstrated proficiency across multiple programming languages, tools, and platforms, showcasing a robust understanding of game development practices and adaptability to experimental workflows.
